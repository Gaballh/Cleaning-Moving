What is included in a seasonal clean-up?

spring cleaning

A spring cleaning means that we prepare your garden for the arrival of spring. We cut down perennials and withered plants, clear the garden from leaves and branches, cut the grass and clear weeds. You can also add more services such as removal and pruning, see below.

Please let us know when booking if you would like to add additional requests so that we can adjust the time and make sure the team have the right equipment.

    Additional services:
  • We prune fruit trees, bushes, and hedges
  • We fight moss in lawns
  • We clean, rake and sweep walkways
  • We prune ornamental shrubs
  • We provide fertilizers for lawns/hedges/garden lands

Autumn cleaning

Autumn cleaning means that we clean the garden before autumn! We clear the garden from leaves and branches, cut the grass and clear weeds. You can also add more services such as removal and pruning, see below.

    Additional services:
  • We prune fruit trees, bushes, and hedges
  • We fight moss in lawns
  • We clean, rake and sweep walkways
  • We prune ornamental shrubs
  • We provide fertilizers for lawns/hedges/garden lands
  • We take away garden waste (not eligible for RUT)
