Our cleaning systems

Deep Cleaning

Deep cleaning is a thorough and comprehensive approach to tidying and sanitizing spaces, surpassing the maintenance routine typically performed. It involves meticulous attention to detail, targeting often overlooked areas and surfaces.

The primary goal of deep cleaning is to eliminate accumulated dirt, dust, and germs, promoting a healthier and more aesthetically pleasing environment.

The best equipment is used for cleaning.

Intensive cleaning

intensive cleaning involves a more thorough examination and treatment of every nook and cranny, focusing on areas that may be neglected during everyday maintenance. This method often includes deep cleaning, disinfecting, and decluttering to achieve a higher level of cleanliness and hygiene. Intensive cleaning is particularly beneficial for maintaining optimal health standards, whether in a residential setting, a workspace, or even in specialized environments like healthcare facilities.

Daily maintenance cleaning

This type of cleaning is essential to prevent the buildup of dirt, dust, and germs, and to promote a clean and healthy environment. Daily maintenance cleaning typically includes activities such as sweeping or vacuuming floors, mopping surfaces, disinfecting commonly touched areas, and ensuring basic order and organization. It helps maintain the overall cleanliness and appearance of the space, making it more comfortable and attractive.
